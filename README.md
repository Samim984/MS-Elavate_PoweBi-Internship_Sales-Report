MS Elevate Power BI Internship – Sales Performance & Product Analytics Dashboard

---

Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the MS Elevate Power BI Internship.
The goal of this project is to build a comprehensive sales analytics dashboard that provides insights into overall business performance, geographical sales distribution, product performance, and salesperson productivity.

The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision making by presenting key metrics, trends, and performance comparisons in an interactive and visually intuitive way.

---

Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Overview



Provides a high-level summary of the business performance.

Key insights:

Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Profit Margin %, Units Sold

Sales trend analysis across time

Revenue distribution by country

Top performing product subcategories

Revenue contribution by product category

2. Geographical Analysis

Analyzes sales performance across different regions and countries.

Key insights:

Country-wise sales and profit performance

Target vs Achievement analysis

Country ranking based on revenue

Regional sales trends

Channel contribution by geography

3. Product Performance

Evaluates product profitability and demand patterns.

Key insights:

Product economics (AOV, Profit per Order, Realization %, Cost Ratio)

Product demand trends over time

Product ranking based on revenue and profit

Profitability analysis using scatter plots

Revenue concentration analysis using Pareto chart

4. Salesperson Performance

Analyzes internal sales team performance and reseller channel contribution.

Key insights:

Total sales, profit, orders, and average sales per representative

Sales distribution by channel (Reseller vs Internet)

Sales by reseller business type

Sales target achievement

Top performing sales representatives

Top performing reseller partners
